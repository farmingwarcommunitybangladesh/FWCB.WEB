Community Codex

Essential guidelines for a fair and organized gaming environment.

General Guidelines

  • Respect all community members regardless of their level.
  • No spamming or trolling in official channels.
  • Use English or Bengali for communication.
  • Participate actively in Clan Wars and Games.
  • Follow your specific clan's requirements.

Farming War Base Rules

Town Hall Section: Must include TH, Eagle, Air Defenses, Sweepers, and all Storages. Must be accessible.

King Section: Must have King, Cannons, Mortars, X-Bows (Ground), and Empty CC.

Queen Section: Queen, Warden, RC, Scattershots, Inferno Towers (Single), and Teslas.

Trap Section: Fully walled area containing ALL traps (except Teslas).
